Size Guide

All T-shirts are a premium unisex fit. Please review measurements carefully as all sales are final.

Chest (Width)

Measure under your arms, around the fullest part of your chest. Keep the tape measure horizontal.

Body Length

Measure from the highest point of the shoulder (beside the collar) down to the bottom hem.
